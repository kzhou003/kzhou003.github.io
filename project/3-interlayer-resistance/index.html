Interlayer resistivity of misoriented MoS$_2$

 

Dec. 2014-Feb. 2017
Authors: Kuan Zhou, Darshana Wickramaratne, Supeng Ge, Shanshan Su, Amrit De, Roger K. Lake

The electron and hole interlayer resistance of misoriented bilayer MoS$_2$ were determined as a function of the misorientation angle. The approach employed electronic structure calculations using the Vienna Ab initio simulation package (VASP) combined with NEGF calculations of the interlayer resistivity. I found that the electron and hole resistivities differed by several orders of magnitude depending on the rotation angle, and how this property could be exploited in the design of a high-frequency bipolar junction transistor or heterostructure bipolar transistor. This work has been published in Physical Chemistry Chemical Physics as an Advanced article.
